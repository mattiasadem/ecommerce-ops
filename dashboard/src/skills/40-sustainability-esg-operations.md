## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the operator is doing **≥$500k US-DTC GMV** with a US / EU / UK / CA / AU customer base and the sustainability-curve has started to compound: Gen-Z + Millennial customer acquisition is increasingly sustainability-driven (canonical Mckinsey 2024 + BCG 2024 + Edelman 2024 + GlobeScan 2024 + NielsenIQ 2024 benchmarks show 60-75% of Gen-Z + Millennial customers consider sustainability-in-purchasing-decision), the brand has been asked by Faire / Ankorstore / Shopify Markets EU / Amazon Climate Pledge Friendly / 1% for the Planet / B-Corp-partner-directory about sustainability-certification, the brand is launching on a 2nd marketplace (Amazon / Walmart / TikTok Shop / Faire / Target Plus / EU marketplaces) and the launch is blocked on "we need sustainability-certification for the marketplace sustainability-tag," the brand is doing ≥1,000 SKUs across ≥3 categories and the merchandising team cannot answer "which 20 SKUs have Bluesign / Oeko-Tex / GRS / FSC / RDS / RWS / Fair Trade / Rainforest Alliance / Leather Working Group / CanopyStyle / B Corp / Cosmos Organic / Ecocert / USDA Organic / Informed Sport certification" within 60 seconds, the brand is doing ≥1 locale in EU / UK / DACH / Nordics and the EU Green Claims Directive 2026 (in force March 2026) is mandatory compliance for any "eco-friendly" / "carbon-neutral" / "biodegradable" / "sustainable" / "recycled" / "compostable" / "natural" claim without third-party-verified-substantiation, the brand has had ≥1 marketplace-suspension / customer-complaint / regulatory-investigation / greenwashing-lawsuit in the last 12 months due to sustainability-claim issues, the brand is doing ≥$500k wholesale-channel-revenue via Faire / Ankorstore / Handshake / RSP / KeHE / UNFI and the wholesale-channel-buyer-bases are asking for sustainability-certification, or the operator cannot answer "what is our sustainability-segment-cohort-LTV vs Default-voice-cohort-LTV" within 30 seconds. Move #27 is the canonical **sustainability-operating layer** the operator needs after Move #1 (cart-abandon) + Move #5 (Klaviyo) + Move #6 (attribution) + Move #9 (mobile PDP) + Move #11 (subscription) + Move #12 (3PL) ship. Without Move #27, the brand caps at "we use recycled mailers and 1% of revenue goes to charity" claim-and-hope — a level of sustainability-claim-substantiation that the EU Green Claims Directive 2026 + California Prop 65 + FTC Green Guides + UK CBAM 2027 all explicitly prohibit without third-party-verified-certification.

**Pitfall #2 — No EU-Green-Claims-Directive-2026 compliance-substrate for EU-active brands.** EU Green Claims Directive 2026 (in force March 2026) explicitly prohibits any "eco-friendly" / "carbon-neutral" / "biodegradable" / "sustainable" / "recycled" / "compostable" / "natural" claim without third-party-verified-substantiation.
