## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the operator is doing **≥$1M US-DTC GMV** with ≥8 source-systems (Shopify + Klaviyo + Postscript + Triple Whale + Recharge + Loop + Gorgias + Smile + ShipBob + Meta-Ads + Google-Ads + TikTok-Ads + Snap-Ads + Pinterest-Ads) and the data-fragmentation curve has started to compound: cross-channel identity-match-rate is <70% (Triple Whale shows 60% match-rate while Klaviyo shows 40% match-rate on the SAME customer-list), Meta CAPI event-match-quality is <85% (Meta Events Manager shows 60-75% match-quality while Triple Whale shows 88%+), Google Enhanced Conversions quality tier is "Needs improvement" or "Good" (not "Excellent"), the operator cannot answer "what is the LTV of customers acquired via paid-social vs organic vs SMS vs email vs affiliate vs creator vs TikTok vs wholesale" within 60 seconds, the operator cannot answer "which 100 customers should I add to the loyalty-tier-up email-flow" within 60 seconds, the operator cannot answer "what is the 30/60/90/180/365-day cohort-LTV of customers who received the welcome-flow vs those who did not" within 60 seconds, the operator cannot answer "what is the per-cohort NPS-score-distribution and the per-cohort LTV-correlation" within 60 seconds, the operator is paying for Triple Whale + Klaviyo + Postscript + Recharge + Loop + Gorgias + Smile but they are NOT sharing a common customer-identity (each system has its own customer-ID + email + phone + first-touch-source + last-touch-source + LTV-band — the systems do NOT agree on the SAME customer), the brand has had ≥2 attribution-disagreement incidents in the last 12 months (Triple Whale says a customer is from paid-social, Klaviyo says email, Meta says paid-social, Google says direct), the brand has had ≥2 Klaviyo-flow-firing-incidents (e.g. welcome-flow-fires-3-times-for-the-same-customer because Triple Whale + Klaviyo + Recharge each have a different email + phone + customer-ID for the same person), the brand has had ≥1 Meta-CAPI-event-quality-disqualification (Meta-Ads account flagged for low-event-match-quality → CPM-15%-50%-lift-penalty), the brand has had ≥1 Google-Enhanced-Conversions-quality-tier-disqualification (Google-Ads account flagged for low-quality-tier → conversion-credit-loss + smart-bidding-penalty), the brand is doing ≥500 SKUs across ≥3 channels and the merchandising team cannot answer "which 100 SKUs were purchased by customers in the sustainability-cohort-LTV-band" within 60 seconds, the brand is doing ≥$500k wholesale-channel-revenue via Faire / Ankorstore / Handshake / RSP / KeHE / UNFI and the wholesale-channel-buyer-bases are asking for per-channel LTV-and-cohort-360-data, or the operator cannot answer "what is the per-customer identity-resolution match-rate across Shopify + Klaviyo + Postscript + Triple Whale + Recharge + Loop + Gorgias + Smile" within 30 seconds.
