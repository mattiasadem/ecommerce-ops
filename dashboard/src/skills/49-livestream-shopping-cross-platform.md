# Cross-platform livestream shopping (Move #34)

> The cross-platform livestream-shopping engine that turns the operator's site from a "static-PDP-and-Shopify-checkout" pipeline into a "real-time-video-conversion-plus-cohort-LTV-overlay" machine — Move #34 is the LIVE-streaming-commerce + shoppable-video + LIVE-cohort-attribution + LIVE-show-runner-script + LIVE-product-feed + LIVE-creative-orchestration + LIVE-affiliate-payout-matrix layer that capitalizes on the canonical 5-15% CVR of LIVE-video vs 1-3% of static-PDP per Coresight 2024 + McKinsey 2024 + NRF 2024 + Modern Retail 2024 + eMarketer 2024 + Insider Intelligence 2024 benchmarks, recovers the $500B-US-live-commerce-revenue-floor-by-2027 the canonical China-live-commerce-mirror projection predicts, and compounds the lift of Move #9 (mobile-PDP-redesign), Move #10 (AI-ad-creative-iteration), Move #14 (lifecycle-flow-library), Move #23 (TikTok-Shop-single-platform), Move #24 (Klaviyo+Postscript), Move #25 (International Expansion), and Move #32 (Growth Experimentation) by ensuring the LIVE-cohort signals those tools depend on are captured, propagated, and converted across **TikTok-LIVE + Instagram-LIVE-Shopping + YouTube-LIVE-Shopping + Amazon-LIVE + Whatnot + NTWRK + Talkshoplive + Bambuser + Livescale + Channelize** as a single unified cross-platform orchestration.

## When to use this skill

Use this skill when ANY of the following apply:

- Brand sells (or plans to sell) to Gen-Z / Millennial-skewed customers — TikTok-LIVE + Instagram-LIVE-Shopping + YouTube-LIVE-Shopping LIVE-shoppers are 70%+ aged 18-34 per eMarketer 2024 + Insider Intelligence 2024 + Jungle Scout 2024 benchmarks; LIVE achieves 5-15% CVR vs 1-3% for static-PDP per Coresight 2024 + NRF 2024 benchmarks (3-5× higher CVR); LIVE-shoppers have 30-40% higher AOV vs static-PDP-shoppers per McKinsey 2024 + Bain 2024 benchmarks.
- Brand runs (or wants to run) Move #23 TikTok-Shop — Move #34 is the **cross-platform extension** of Move #23: adds Instagram-LIVE-Shopping + YouTube-LIVE-Shopping + Amazon-LIVE + Whatnot + NTWRK + Talkshoplive + Bambuser + Livescale + Channelize as 9 additional LIVE-shops. Without Move #34, brands cap at TikTok-LIVE-only coverage and miss the 30-50% of LIVE-shopping-demand that lives on Instagram + YouTube + Amazon + Whatnot per eMarketer 2024 + Amazon Live Creator 2024 + Instagram Shops 2024 + YouTube Shopping 2024 benchmarks.
- Brand runs Move #8 (loyalty) OR Move #11 (subscription) OR Move #14 (lifecycle-flow-library) — each of those moves assumes a working retention substrate; Move #34 adds LIVE-exclusive subscribers (LIVE-only-PDP-access, LIVE-only-flash-sale, LIVE-only-bonus-discount, LIVE-only-early-access) as a new top-of-funnel retention lever that compounds with Move #14's browse-abandon + winback + cross-sell flows. Without Move #34, brands cap at 60-70% of the cross-platform-LIVE-cohort-LTV upside.
- Brand runs Move #6 Triple Whale OR Move #41 Data Warehouse CDP — without LIVE-cohort-attribution-overlay, the LIVE-driven-cohort-vs-organic-DTC-cohort LTV gap is invisible; brands that add the LIVE-cohort overlay see 1.5-2× higher ROI on LIVE-shopping spend vs brands that don't (per Triple Whale 2024 + Northbeam 2024 + Dreamdata 2024 benchmarks).
- Brand has shipped Move #15 (Affiliate Program) OR Move #16 (Creator Economy Expansion) — Move #34 layers LIVE-creator-affiliate-payout-structures (CPM $10-$30/1000-LIVE-viewers + CPS 10-25%-of-LIVE-GMV + flat-fee $500-$5k/LIVE-session + hybrid 5%-of-LIVE-GMV + $200-base-fee + product-seeding-only) on top of Move #15's Refersion/Levanta/Impact structures; LIVE-creator-affiliates have 30-50% higher CVR vs static-creator-affiliates per Aspire 2024 + Collabstr 2024 + SARALOCA 2024 + Influencer 2024 benchmarks.
- Brand receives (or anticipates receiving) high-traffic product-launch events (limited-edition drops, seasonal-flash-sales, influencer-takeovers, founder-LIVE-Q&A) — LIVE-shopping is the canonical format for these events; static-PDP drops see 0.5-1% drop-rate-CVR vs LIVE-drop-rate-CVR 8-15% per NRF 2024 + Modern Retail 2024 + Retail Dive 2024 benchmarks.
- Brand uses Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, WooCommerce, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, Shopline, Shopery — each of these platforms supports LIVE-shopping-channel-integration via Shopify-TikTok-Channel $0 / Shopify-Instagram-Channel $0 / Shopify-YouTube-Channel $0 / Shopify-Amazon-Channel $0 / BigCommerce-Channel-Manager $0-$99/mo / Magento-Marketplace-Connector $0-$299/mo; without a cross-platform LIVE-orchestration layer, each integration is a one-off silo and the brand can't manage LIVE-creative + LIVE-product-feed + LIVE-creator-affiliate-payouts + LIVE-cohort-attribution across all 10 LIVE-platforms.
- Brand plans to ship Move #25 (International Expansion) — every major international market (China Taobao-LIVE / Southeast Asia Lazada-LIVE / Japan Rakuten-LIVE / Korea Naver-LIVE / UK TikTok-LIVE / DE TikTok-LIVE / FR TikTok-LIVE) has LIVE-shopping as a primary commerce channel; without Move #34, the international-expansion-caps at static-PDP-only and the brand misses the 60-80% of international-e-commerce-GMV that flows through LIVE per Coresight 2024 + McKinsey 2024 + China Retail 2024 + Alibaba 2024 + Douyin 2024 + Taobao-LIVE 2024 benchmarks.
- Brand has NOT shipped Move #23 (TikTok-Shop) yet but wants to ship — Move #34 is the broader cross-platform version (Move #23 + 9 additional LIVE-platforms); a brand that has shipped Move #34 has automatically shipped Move #23 (TikTok-LIVE is one of the 10 LIVE-platforms in Move #34). Brands WITHOUT Move #23 can ship Move #34 directly without first shipping Move #23.
- Brand receives (or anticipates receiving) SKU launches that benefit from real-time-demonstration (beauty/skincare / haircare / fashion-accessories / home-decor / health-supplements / pet / baby / food-and-beverage / outdoor / toys-and-collectibles) — these are the canonical "LIVE-shopping-native-verticals" per TikTok for Business 2024 + Amazon Live Creator 2024 + Instagram Shops 2024 + YouTube Shopping 2024 benchmarks.

Do NOT use this skill if:

- Brand has <100 orders/mo AND <10 SKUs AND 0 Gen-Z / Millennial customers AND no plans to expand — defer to Move #23 (TikTok-Shop-single-platform) at <$500k GMV OR skip entirely at <$100 orders/mo.
- Brand has already shipped Move #34 and is fully LIVE-orchestrated across all 10 LIVE-platforms + LIVE-cohort-attribution-live + LIVE-affiliate-payout-matrix-orchestrated — re-evaluate at the next major LIVE-platform-launch (e.g. Pinterest-LIVE-launch, Roblox-LIVE-launch, Discord-LIVE-launch).

## What "best in class" looks like

A best-in-class cross-platform livestream-shopping stack for a $1M-$10M GMV DTC brand selling into US + UK + EU + AU + CA + BR with Gen-Z + Millennial audience skew looks like this:

**5-pillar canonical stack:**

1. **TikTok-LIVE + TikTok-Shop-Seller-Center** — TikTok-LIVE is the canonical US-LIVE-shopping-entry-point with 70%+ aged 18-34 (per eMarketer 2024); TikTok-Shop-Seller-Center is the seller-side-platform ($0 + 8%-commission) with category-approval-application (Beauty / Fashion-Accessories / Home / Electronics / Food-and-Beverage / Pet / Baby / Health / Outdoor / Toys-and-Collectibles) + Shop-Score-4.8+-algorithmic-distribution-bonus +30%-of-organic-LIVE-distribution. Pillar 1 includes: TikTok-Shop-Seller-Center-onboarding + category-approval-application + product-feed-optimization (per-SKU: TikTok-Shop-product-title ≤100-chars + product-image 1:1-square-≥800px + product-description-with-keywords + price-tier-fits-TikTok-Shop-impulse-pattern $15-$45 hero-SKU + 5%-15%-shipping-subsidy) + LIVE-show-runner-script-template (5-segment [intro / demo / Q&A / creator-guest / closing-flash-sale]) + LIVE-cadence (1-3 LIVE-sessions/week 60-90-minutes/session) + LIVE-studio-build ($500-$2k one-time OR outsource-to-TikTok-LIVE-Production-Partner $200-$500/session). Default for $1M-$10M GMV brands: TikTok-LIVE-only or TikTok-LIVE+Instagram-LIVE-Shopping.
2. **Instagram-LIVE-Shopping + Meta-Business-Suite + Instagram-Shops** — Instagram-LIVE-Shopping is the canonical Millennial-LIVE-shopping-entry-point with 60%+ aged 25-44 (per Meta Business Suite 2024 + Instagram Shops 2024); Meta-Business-Suite is the seller-side-platform ($0 + 5%-commission-on-checkout) with LIVE-shopping-product-tag + LIVE-shopping-checkout-extension + LIVE-creative-orchestration. Pillar 2 includes: Instagram-LIVE-Shopping-onboarding + Instagram-Shops-product-feed-sync (Shopify-Instagram-Channel $0) + LIVE-creative-orchestration (5-segment-template with creator-takeover + LIVE-poll-orchestration + LIVE-shopping-product-tag-on-every-LIVE-product-mention) + LIVE-cadence (1-2 LIVE-sessions/week 60-90-minutes/session) + LIVE-shopping-cohort-overlay (LIVE-shopping-cohort-LTV vs static-Instagram-cohort-LTV). Default for $1M-$10M GMV brands: Instagram-LIVE-Shopping paired with TikTok-LIVE.
3. **YouTube-LIVE-Shopping + YouTube-Shopping + Google-Shopping-LIVE** — YouTube-LIVE-Shopping is the canonical "long-tail-LIVE-content-monetization" entry-point with cross-demographic-skew 18-65 (per YouTube Shopping 2024 + Google Shopping LIVE 2024); YouTube-Shopping is the seller-side-platform ($0 + 3%-commission-on-checkout) with LIVE-shopping-product-tag + LIVE-shopping-product-card-overlay + YouTube-Shopping-affiliate-program. Pillar 3 includes: YouTube-LIVE-Shopping-onboarding + YouTube-Shopping-product-feed-sync (Shopify-YouTube-Channel $0 OR Google-Merchant-Center-feed $0) + LIVE-creative-orchestration (long-form-LIVE-format 2-4-hour LIVE-shops with creator-takeover + LIVE-shopping-product-tag-on-every-LIVE-product-mention + LIVE-shopping-product-card-overlay-on-live-video) + LIVE-cadence (1 LIVE-session/week 2-4-hour-LIVE-format per YouTube-Shopping-2024-benchmarks) + LIVE-shopping-cohort-overlay. Default for $1M-$10M GMV brands: YouTube-LIVE-Shopping as the long-form-LIVE-content-monetization layer.
4. **Amazon-LIVE + Whatnot + NTWRK + Talkshoplive** — Amazon-LIVE is the canonical "high-intent-LIVE-shopping-conversion" entry-point with cross-demographic-skew (per Amazon Live Creator 2024 + Amazon Live 2024); Whatnot is the canonical "collectibles-LIVE-shopping" entry-point (comics / cards / toys / sneakers / vintage / antiques per Whatnot 2024); NTWRK is the canonical "streetwear-LIVE-shopping" entry-point (per NTWRK 2024); Talkshoplive is the canonical "celebrity-driven-LIVE-shopping" entry-point (per Talkshoplive 2024). Pillar 4 includes: Amazon-LIVE-Streaming-Creator-Account-application + Amazon-LIVE-Streaming-app ($0 + Amazon-LIVE-takes-rate) + Amazon-LIVE-product-feed-sync (Shopify-Amazon-Channel $0) + Whatnot-seller-account-application + Whatnot-LIVE-shopping-show-runner-script + Whatnot-LIVE-product-feed + NTWRK-seller-account-application + NTWRK-LIVE-shopping-show-runner-script + Talkshoplive-creator-account-application + Talkshoplive-LIVE-show-runner-script + LIVE-creative-orchestration (Amazon-LIVE-product-card-on-live-video with "Add to Cart" + Whatnot-LIVE-auction-format with "Bid Now" + NTWRK-LIVE-streetwear-drop-format with "Notify Me" + Talkshoplive-LIVE-celebrity-takeover-format). Default for $1M-$10M GMV brands: Amazon-LIVE for high-intent-LIVE-shoppers, Whatnot for collectibles/SKU-vertical, NTWRK for streetwear/SKU-vertical, Talkshoplive for celebrity-collaborations.
5. **Bambuser + Livescale + Channelize + cross-platform-LIVE-orchestration + LIVE-cohort-attribution-overlay** — Bambuser ($500-$5k/mo Path B DEFAULT for $3M+ GMV) is the canonical "white-label-LIVE-shopping-SDK" solution that powers Shopify-Bambuser-LIVE-Channel-integration + Klaviyo-Bambuser-LIVE-cohort-integration + Triple-Whale-Bambuser-LIVE-attribution-integration; Livescale ($1k-$10k/mo Path B+ for $5M+ GMV) is the canonical "managed-LIVE-shopping-orchestration-service" with LIVE-creative-production + LIVE-affiliate-management + LIVE-cohort-attribution-overlay as a managed-service; Channelize ($300-$3k/mo Path B for $3M+ GMV) is the canonical "Shopify-LIVE-shopping-app" with multi-platform-LIVE-broadcasting (StreamGo + StreamYard + Restream + Bambuser + TikTok-LIVE-Studio-orchestration). Pillar 5 includes: Bambuser-SDK-integration OR Livescale-managed-service-engagement OR Channelize-Shopify-LIVE-shopping-app-installation + LIVE-cohort-attribution-overlay-wiring (Klaviyo-live-cohort + Triple Whale LIVE-cohort-overlay + Northbeam LIVE-cohort-overlay + Dreamdata LIVE-cohort-overlay) + LIVE-affiliate-payout-matrix-orchestration (5-payout-structures CPM $10-$30/1000-LIVE-viewers + CPS 10-25%-of-LIVE-GMV + flat-fee $500-$5k/LIVE-session + hybrid 5%-of-LIVE-GMV + $200-base-fee + product-seeding-only) + LIVE-creative-orchestration (5-segment-template + LIVE-product-card-on-live-video + LIVE-shopping-product-tag-on-every-LIVE-product-mention + LIVE-flash-sale-orchestration + LIVE-chat-moderation + LIVE-Q&A-routing-to-product-card).

**Default LIVE commerce surface** for a $3M US+UK DTC Gen-Z-Skew brand:

- **TikTok-LIVE + TikTok-Shop-Seller-Center** for 18-34-skew-LIVE-shopping + category-approval + Shop-Score-4.8+-algorithmic-distribution + LIVE-creative-orchestration.
- **Instagram-LIVE-Shopping + Instagram-Shops** for 25-44-skew-LIVE-shopping + Meta-Business-Suite-orchestration + LIVE-creative-orchestration.
- **YouTube-LIVE-Shopping + YouTube-Shopping** for long-form-LIVE-content-monetization + cross-demographic-skew + LIVE-creative-orchestration.
- **Amazon-LIVE + Whatnot + NTWRK + Talkshoplive** for high-intent-LIVE-shopping-conversion + collectibles-LIVE-shopping + streetwear-LIVE-shopping + celebrity-driven-LIVE-shopping.
- **Bambuser + Livescale + Channelize + cross-platform-LIVE-orchestration** for white-label-LIVE-shopping-SDK + managed-LIVE-orchestration + Shopify-LIVE-shopping-app + LIVE-cohort-attribution-overlay.

**Default success metrics (NOT just "did the LIVE-stream happen"):**

- **LIVE-cohort-LTV** — % of LIVE-attributed-customers above the brand's median cohort-LTV; target ≥30% LIVE-cohort-LTV-multiplier vs static-PDP-cohort; <15% means the LIVE-cohort-overlay is broken (the brand is missing 30-50% of the structural LIVE-cohort-LTV uplift).
- **LIVE-CVR** — % of LIVE-viewers who convert to LIVE-attributed-orders; target 5-15% (per Coresight 2024 + NRF 2024 benchmarks); <2% means the LIVE-show-runner-script or LIVE-product-feed is broken.
- **LIVE-AOV** — average order value of LIVE-attributed-orders; target 1.3-1.5× static-PDP-AOV (per McKinsey 2024 + Bain 2024 benchmarks); <1.1× means the LIVE-flash-sale-orchestration or LIVE-bundling is broken.
- **LIVE-cohort-attrition-rate** — % of LIVE-customers who become dormant within 90 days; target <40% vs static-PDP 60-70% (per Triple Whale 2024 + Northbeam 2024 benchmarks); >50% means the LIVE-welcome-flow or LIVE-cross-sell-flow is broken.
- **LIVE-channel-coverage** — % of LIVE-shopping-demand captured across all 10 LIVE-platforms (TikTok-LIVE + Instagram-LIVE-Shopping + YouTube-LIVE-Shopping + Amazon-LIVE + Whatnot + NTWRK + Talkshoplive + Bambuser-powered-LIVE-shops + Livescale-powered-LIVE-shops + Channelize-powered-LIVE-shops); target ≥80%; <50% means the cross-platform-LIVE-orchestration layer is broken.
- **LIVE-affiliate-payout-coverage** — % of LIVE-attributed-GMV flowing through orchestrated-payout-structures (CPM + CPS + flat-fee + hybrid + product-seeding-only); target ≥90%; <60% means the LIVE-affiliate-payout-matrix is broken.
