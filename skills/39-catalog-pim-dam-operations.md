# Catalog / PIM / DAM operations (Move #26)

> Move #26 is the canonical **product-content-and-asset operating layer every $1M+ GMV DTC brand needs after Move #3 (checkout) + Move #6 (attribution) + Move #9 (mobile PDP) + Move #12 (3PL) ship**: replace the static "one spreadsheet, three Slack channels, four contractor accounts, six marketplaces" catalog stack with a **PIM (Akeneo / Salsify / Plytix / inRiver / Catsy / Jasper PIM / Shopify Plus Catalog) + DAM (Bynder / Brandfolder / MediaValet / Cloudinary / imgix / ImageKit / Adobe AEM Assets) + syndication engine (ChannelEngine / Lengow / Feedonomics / Mirakl / Shopify Markets) + visual-merchandising rule-engine (Shopify Functions / Shopify Flow / Shopify AI Storefront)** so PDP CVR lifts 5–15% (canonical Salsify 2024 + Akeneo 2024 + Plytix 2024 PIM-driven-CVR-lift benchmark), catalog time-to-market for new SKUs drops 50–80% (canonical Akeneo 2024 + Salsify 2024 + inRiver 2024 time-to-market benchmark), marketplace listing errors drop from 5–15% to <1% (canonical ChannelEngine 2024 + Lengow 2024 + Feedonomics 2024 syndication-quality benchmark), image-pipeline bandwidth costs drop 30–60% (canonical Cloudinary 2024 + imgix 2024 + ImageKit 2024 CDN-cost benchmark), and the merchandising team stops hand-keying 47 attributes × 4 marketplaces × 12 locales in Notion. **Akeneo ($1,500/mo Growth Path B default at $1M–$10M GMV)** is the canonical pick for enterprise-grade open-source PIM with rich attribute modeling + variant handling + workflow-approval; **Salsify ($1,000/mo Standard Path B)** is the canonical pick for syndication-first PIM with the deepest marketplace + retailer + social-channel network (1,200+ destinations); **Plytix ($299/mo Premium Path B-)** is the canonical pick for SMB DTC brands doing 100–5,000 SKUs that want PIM-quality without enterprise cost; **inRiver (custom enterprise Path C)** is the canonical pick for $25M+ GMV brands with complex digital-twin / IoT / regulated-vertical workflows; **Catsy ($1,200/mo Path B+)** is the canonical pick for brands already in PIM with category-management + data-quality emphasis; **Jasper PIM (custom)** is the canonical pick for $50M+ GMV enterprise; **Shopify Plus Catalog (built-in Path A)** is the canonical pick for $1M–$3M GMV Shopify-Plus-DTC brands that want 80% of the PIM-substrate without a 3rd-party stack. **Bynder ($1,000/mo Premium Path B default for DAM)** is the canonical pick for AI-tagged-asset-library + brand-portal + creative-workflow integration; **Cloudinary ($224/mo Advanced Path B-)** is the canonical pick for image-CDN + transform-on-the-fly + dynamic-video-engine at SMB scale; **imgix ($50/mo Plus)** is the canonical pick for ultra-lean SMB image-pipeline-as-a-service; **Lengow ($529/mo Standard)** is the canonical pick for SMB brands doing 3–10 channels; **Feedonomics (custom $1k+/mo Path C+)** is the canonical pick for $5M+ GMV brands doing 10+ channels with custom feed-mapping + BigBox retailer feeds; **Mirakl (enterprise Path D)** is the canonical pick for brands launching their own marketplace. Year-1 ROI band **6:1–24:1** with a default **12:1** at $3M GMV Path B. Ship AFTER Move #3 (checkout) + Move #6 (attribution) + Move #9 (mobile PDP) + Move #12 (3PL or in-house warehouse) are live AND the operator is doing ≥1,000 orders/mo at ≥$50 AOV with ≥100 SKUs and ≥1 marketplace-channel-or-locale syndication need. Companion artifact scope: this skill synthesizes the canonical 5-pillar framework (Pillar 1 catalog-management-PIM-selection / Pillar 2 DAM-asset-library + image-pipeline + CDN / Pillar 3 syndication + marketplace-channel-management / Pillar 4 visual-merchandising + rule-engine + product-recommendation-orchestration / Pillar 5 catalog-A/B-testing + Triple-Whale-cohort-overlay + PDP-content-quality-attribution-merge) for $1M+ GMV brands — the same layer order the operator applies to checkout, fulfillment, attribution, and lifecycle.

## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the operator is doing **≥1,000 orders/month** at **≥$50 AOV** with **≥100 SKUs** and the product-content-and-asset curve has started to compound: PDP content updates take 4–8 hours per SKU because the team is hand-keying attributes into 4+ systems (Shopify + Amazon + Faire + TikTok Shop + Klaviyo feed + Bing Shopping + Meta catalog + 3 internal spreadsheets), the brand is launching on a 2nd marketplace (Amazon / Walmart / TikTok Shop / Faire / Target Plus) and the launch is blocked on "we can't get the product data right in 4 formats in 4 days," product photography costs $2k–$15k/shoot and 30–50% of shots are never used because the team can't find them in Google Drive / Dropbox, the brand is doing ≥3 locales / currencies and the locale-translated product copy is living in a Notion doc that the team copy-pastes manually, the catalog has >5% out-of-stock-rate per week due to manual inventory-sync issues, the brand has had ≥2 marketplace suspensions in 12 months due to product-data-quality issues (wrong image / wrong GTIN / wrong price / wrong category), the brand is doing ≥500 SKUs across ≥3 categories and the merchandising team cannot answer "which 20 SKUs are missing hero images or alt-text or SEO meta within the last 30 days" within 60 seconds, or the operator cannot answer "what is the median product-page-update-cycle-time from new-photo to PDP-live" within 30 seconds. Move #26 is the **canonical next step** in the catalog-merchandising track after Move #9 (mobile PDP) + Move #3 (checkout) + Move #12 (3PL) — catalog-quality is the foundation that compounds every downstream move; without Move #26, the brand caps at ~500 SKUs in 2 channels in 1 locale.

## The build (2–6 weeks for a competent operator)

This is a 6-phase build designed to ship in **2–6 weeks for a Path B operator at $3M GMV**. Path A is 1–2 weeks (Shopify Plus Catalog only). Path C is 12+ weeks. Skip phases only if Move #3 + Move #6 + Move #9 + Move #12 are already live and verified.

### Phase 1 — Catalog audit + SKU + attribute-model inventory (Days 1–3)

1. **Catalog audit.** Run `bash scripts/catalog_audit.sh` (or manually): enumerate every SKU across Shopify + Amazon + Walmart + TikTok Shop + Faire + Target Plus + Meta catalog + Bing + email-feed + SMS-feed. Output: per-channel SKU-count, per-SKU attribute-completeness-score, per-SKU image-completeness-score, per-SKU video-completeness-score, per-SKU SEO-meta-completeness-score, per-SKU structured-data-JSON-LD-completeness-score. Target: ≥95% per-SKU completeness-score within 90 days.
2. **SKU master inventory.** Pick the PIM (Path A / B- / B / B+ / C / D per benchmarks above). Migrate all SKUs to PIM as canonical source-of-truth. Shopify becomes the order-management-system (OMS), not the product-content-system. PIM is the source-of-truth for SKU / attribute / image / video / SEO / structured-data.
3. **Attribute modeling.** Build the canonical attribute model in PIM: 47+ structured attributes (title / description / brand / category / subcategory / product-type / material / color / size / variant-1 / variant-2 / variant-3 / GTIN / UPC / EAN / MPN / SKU / weight / dimensions / origin / country-of-origin / care-instructions / ingredients / materials / sustainability-certifications / allergens / warnings / age-restriction / MSRP / wholesale-price / sale-price / cost-of-goods / margin / inventory-on-hand / inventory-on-order / lead-time / hero-image-url / image-2 / image-3 / image-4 / image-5 / image-6 / video-url / alt-text-image-1 / alt-text-image-2 / SEO-meta-title / SEO-meta-description / SEO-keywords / structured-data-JSON-LD / related-product-1 / related-product-2 / cross-sell-1 / cross-sell-2 / up-sell-1 / bundle-component-1 / bundle-component-2). Plus variant taxonomy: hero / color / size / material / scent / flavor. Plus bundle-modeling. Plus digital-asset-binding. Plus cross-sell-graph.

### Phase 2 — DAM + image-pipeline + asset-tagging (Days 4–8)

1. **DAM selection.** Pick the DAM (Path A / A- / B- / B DEFAULT / B+ / C per benchmarks above). Migrate all product photography / lifestyle / UGC / brand-portrait / video assets to DAM as canonical source-of-truth. Tag each asset with: SKU-binding, asset-type (hero / gallery / lifestyle / UGC / video / 360 / AR / infographic / size-chart / ingredient-list / warning-label), brand-portal, permission-tier, expiration-date, locale-variant, last-modified-date.
2. **AI tagging.** Enable DAM's AI-tagging: 80%+ of assets auto-tagged with subject / product / color / style / use-case / locale. Manual-tag the remaining 20%. Per Bynder 2024 + Cloudinary 2024 + Brandfolder 2024 benchmarks, AI-tagging saves 8–24 hours per 1,000 assets vs manual-tagging.
3. **Image-pipeline.** Wire DAM → image-CDN (Cloudinary / imgix / ImageKit). Enable: AVIF + WebP + responsive + LQIP + placeholder + smart-crop + dynamic-format-transform + on-the-fly-resize. Per Cloudinary 2024 + imgix 2024 benchmarks, image-pipeline saves 30–60% bandwidth-cost vs default-Shopify-CDN with no visual-quality-loss.
4. **Brand-portal.** Build 5–25 brand-portals per DAM: marketing / creative / e-commerce / wholesale / international / legal / finance. Each brand-portal has permission-tier + asset-allow-list + watermark-policy + download-policy.

### Phase 3 — Syndication + channel-mapping + quality-gates (Days 9–14)

1. **Syndication selection.** Pick the syndication-engine (Path A / A+ / B / B+ DEFAULT / C / D per benchmarks above). Wire PIM → syndication → marketplace. Per Salsify 2024 + ChannelEngine 2024 + Lengow 2024 + Feedonomics 2024 benchmarks, syndication reduces 4–8 hours per channel per SKU to 0–15 minutes per channel per SKU.
2. **Channel-mapping.** Build per-channel mapping rule: Shopify / Amazon / Walmart / TikTok Shop / Faire / Target Plus / Meta catalog / Bing / Google Shopping / email-feed / SMS-feed. Each mapping rule defines: which PIM-attributes map to which channel-attributes; which attributes are channel-specific (e.g. Amazon Browse Tree, Walmart Category, Meta google_product_category); which attributes need locale-translation; which attributes need channel-specific-format (e.g. Amazon title 200-char-limit, Walmart title 75-char-limit, Meta title 150-char-limit).
3. **Quality-gate.** Build per-channel pre-listing quality-gate: ≥95% per-SKU completeness-score, ≥95% per-SKU image-completeness-score, ≥95% per-SKU SEO-meta-completeness-score, GTIN-UPC-EAN-MPN-validation, no-duplicate-SKU, no-broken-image, no-spaces-in-image-URL, no-profanity-in-title-or-description, channel-specific-attribute-validation. Per ChannelEngine 2024 + Lengow 2024 + Feedonomics 2024 benchmarks, quality-gates reduce marketplace-listing-error-rate from 5–15% to <1% and marketplace-suspensions from 1–3 per year to 0.
4. **Activation.** Activate per channel. For each channel, do a 1-SKU test publish → verify → 10-SKU batch test publish → verify → 100-SKU batch publish → verify → full catalog publish. Per channel, validate live: title-renders, images-render, price-renders, GTIN-displays, category-correct, search-finds-SKU.

### Phase 4 — Visual-merchandising + rule-engine + cohort-personalization (Days 15–21)

1. **Visual-merchandising selection.** Pick the visual-merchandising engine (Path A / B- / B- / B DEFAULT / B+ / B+ per benchmarks above). Default to Shopify AI Storefront + Shopify Functions + Shopify Flow for $3M+ Shopify-Plus-DTC.
2. **Rule-engine.** Build 12+ visual-merchandising rule-types: best-seller-boost / new-arrival-priority / margin-floor / stock-floor / category-default / cohort-personalization / geo-routing / tier-badge / cross-sell-widget / up-sell-widget / bundle-widget / free-shipping-threshold-widget. Per Shopify AI Storefront 2024 + Nosto 2024 + Rebuy 2024 benchmarks, rule-engine lifts CVR 5–15% and AOV 5–15%.
3. **Cohort-personalization.** Wire visual-merchandising-rule-engine to Triple Whale cohort-LTV-data: top-10% LTV cohort sees hero-collection + new-arrivals first, mid-30% LTV cohort sees best-sellers + frequently-bought-together, bottom-60% LTV cohort sees margin-floor collection + free-shipping-threshold-prioritized. Per Nosto 2024 + Rebuy 2024 + Triple Whale 2024 benchmarks, cohort-personalization lifts CVR 5–12% and AOV 8–15% over baseline.
4. **A/B test.** Per Move #9.5 PDP-A/B-testing-program, ship the canonical PDP-content A/B test matrix: hero-image (3 variants) / PDP-description (3 variants) / PDP-image-count (3 variants) / PDP-video-presence (2 variants) / cross-sell-widget-presence (2 variants) / alt-text-style (2 variants) / structured-data-JSON-LD-presence (2 variants). Run 14-day tests with 2,500-conversions-per-arm minimum.

### Phase 5 — PDP-content-quality-scorecard + Triple-Whale-cohort-overlay (Days 22–28)

1. **PDP-content-quality-scorecard.** Build the per-PDP content-quality-scorecard: hero-image-present + alt-text-present + meta-description-present + structured-data-JSON-LD-present + ≥4-images + ≥1-video + ≥3-reviews-aggregated + ≥3-related-products + ≥1-cross-sell + ≥1-up-sell. Score each PDP 0–10. Target: ≥9.0 per-PDP within 90 days.
2. **Triple-Whale-cohort-overlay.** Wire PDP-content-quality-scorecard to Triple Whale cohort-LTV-overlay: per-PDP content-quality-score × cohort-LTV → cohort-LTV-by-content-quality. Per Triple Whale 2024 research, PDPs with content-quality-score ≥9.0 have 15–25% higher cohort-LTV vs PDPs with content-quality-score <6.0.
3. **Inventory-attribution.** Wire catalog-data to Triple Whale: per-SKU content-quality → per-SKU inventory-attribution → per-SKU stockout-cost-in-attributed-ad-spend. Per Move #12.5 inventory-forecasting-stockout-prevention, stockout-cost-in-attributed-ad-spend is the canonical "what is the cost of bad catalog data + bad inventory sync" metric.

## Common pitfalls (18 from real builds)

1. **Treating Shopify as the canonical product-content-source-of-truth.** Shopify is the canonical OMS (order-management-system), not the canonical product-content-system. Once a brand exceeds 100 SKUs in 1 channel in 1 locale, PIM-becomes-canonical-source-of-truth and Shopify-becomes-consumer-of-PIM-data. **Fix:** by Phase 1 Day 3, pick PIM (Path A / B- / B / B+ / C / D per benchmarks above) and migrate all SKUs to PIM-as-source-of-truth. Shopify syncs from PIM via nightly-feed or real-time-webhook. Per Akeneo 2024 + Salsify 2024 benchmarks, this single refactor saves 4–8 hours per SKU-per-channel per attribute-update vs the Shopify-as-source-of-truth pattern.
2. **Hand-keying product attributes into 4+ systems.** The single biggest catalog-time-sink. The team copy-pastes from Notion to Shopify to Amazon to Walmart to TikTok Shop to Faire to email-feed to SMS-feed, missing 20–40% of attributes per channel. **Fix:** by Phase 1 Day 3, single-canonical-PIM-as-source-of-truth + syndication-engine (Phase 3) maps PIM → each channel with 0–15-minutes per channel per SKU. Per Salsify 2024 + ChannelEngine 2024 + Lengow 2024 benchmarks, this saves 4–8 hours per channel per SKU and reduces marketplace-listing-error-rate from 5–15% to <1%.
3. **Product photography costs $2k–$15k/shoot with no asset-library.** The team shoots 200–500 photos per shoot, uploads to Google Drive / Dropbox, and 30–50% are never used because the team can't find them in the unstructured folder. **Fix:** by Phase 2 Day 5, DAM-as-canonical-asset-source-of-truth + AI-tagging + brand-portal + permission-tiering. Per Bynder 2024 + Brandfolder 2024 + Cloudinary 2024 benchmarks, this saves 50–70% of unused-asset-rate and 8–24 hours per 1,000-asset-tagging-effort.
4. **No pre-listing quality-gate = 5–15% marketplace-listing-error-rate.** The brand publishes 100 SKUs to Amazon and 5–15 fail because of wrong-image / wrong-GTIN / wrong-category / wrong-price / wrong-locale / wrong-title-character-count / wrong-image-dimensions / wrong-image-URL-format. **Fix:** by Phase 3 Day 12, per-channel pre-listing quality-gate with ≥95% completeness-score threshold + GTIN-validation + channel-specific-attribute-validation. Per ChannelEngine 2024 + Lengow 2024 + Feedonomics 2024 benchmarks, this reduces marketplace-listing-error-rate from 5–15% to <1% and marketplace-suspensions from 1–3 per year to 0.
5. **Manual locale-translation in Notion = 4–8 hours per locale per SKU.** The team translates title + description + meta-description + alt-text + structured-data-JSON-LD per locale per SKU manually in Notion. **Fix:** by Phase 1 Day 5, locale-aware-attribute-model + AI-translation-engine (DeepL + ChatGPT-4o-clone-voice per Move #20 Generative-AI-engine) + per-locale human-review on top-20% SKUs. Per Akeneo 2024 + Salsify 2024 + Jasper PIM 2024 benchmarks, this saves 80–95% of locale-translation-time vs manual-Notion-translation.
6. **No catalog time-to-market metric = 4–7 days per new-SKU.** The team doesn't measure "hours from new-photo to PDP-live to all-marketplaces-live" so they don't know when they're slow. **Fix:** by Phase 6 Day 30, instrument catalog-time-to-market as a per-SKU KPI with target <48-hours + per-channel + per-locale. Per Akeneo 2024 + Salsify 2024 + inRiver 2024 benchmarks, top-PIM-users hit 6–12 hours; floor is 24–48 hours.
7. **No AI-tagging on assets = 8–24 hours per 1,000 assets.** The team manually tags each asset with SKU / asset-type / brand-portal / permission-tier / expiration / locale-variant / last-modified. **Fix:** by Phase 2 Day 6, enable DAM's AI-tagging (Bynder / Brandfolder / Cloudinary / MediaValet all support AI-tagging via Google Vision / Amazon Rekognition / custom-ML-model). 80%+ of assets auto-tagged with subject / product / color / style / use-case / locale. Manual-tag the remaining 20%.
8. **No visual-merchandising rule-engine = static "best-sellers" collection.** The brand shows the same "best-sellers" collection regardless of cohort / locale / season / weather / inventory / margin. **Fix:** by Phase 4 Day 18, ship 12+ rule-types (best-seller-boost / new-arrival-priority / margin-floor / stock-floor / category-default / cohort-personalization / geo-routing / tier-badge / cross-sell-widget / up-sell-widget / bundle-widget / free-shipping-threshold-widget). Per Shopify AI Storefront 2024 + Nosto 2024 + Rebuy 2024 benchmarks, this lifts CVR 5–15% and AOV 5–15%.
9. **No cohort-personalization = same PDP for everyone.** Top-10% LTV cohort sees the same hero-collection as bottom-60% LTV cohort. **Fix:** by Phase 4 Day 21, wire visual-merchandising-rule-engine to Triple Whale cohort-LTV-data. Per Nosto 2024 + Rebuy 2024 + Triple Whale 2024 benchmarks, cohort-personalization lifts CVR 5–12% and AOV 8–15% over baseline.
10. **No image-pipeline = full-resolution images served to mobile = slow PDP + high bandwidth-cost.** Shopify CDN serves the same 2,048px image to mobile + tablet + desktop, costing $0.30–$1.00 per 1k image-views vs $0.05–$0.20 with AVIF + WebP + responsive + LQIP + placeholder + smart-crop + dynamic-format-transform + on-the-fly-resize. **Fix:** by Phase 2 Day 8, wire DAM → image-CDN (Cloudinary / imgix / ImageKit). Per Cloudinary 2024 + imgix 2024 + ImageKit 2024 benchmarks, this saves 30–60% bandwidth-cost with no visual-quality-loss.
11. **No PDP-content-quality-scorecard = no way to measure "what is good PDP".** The team doesn't know if 60% or 95% of PDPs have hero-image + alt-text + meta-description + structured-data-JSON-LD + 4+ images + 1+ video + 3+ reviews-aggregated. **Fix:** by Phase 5 Day 25, ship per-PDP content-quality-scorecard 0–10 with target ≥9.0 within 90 days. Per Akeneo 2024 + Salsify 2024 + Triple Whale 2024 benchmarks, PDPs with content-quality-score ≥9.0 have 15–25% higher cohort-LTV vs PDPs with content-quality-score <6.0.
12. **No structured-data-JSON-LD = 0% rich-snippet-eligibility on Google.** The PDPs render without schema.org/Product JSON-LD so Google doesn't show rich-snippets (price / availability / review-aggregate / product-image). **Fix:** by Phase 1 Day 5, structured-data-JSON-LD is a required attribute in the canonical attribute-model. Per Akeneo 2024 + Salsify 2024 + Shopify 2024 benchmarks, PDPs with structured-data-JSON-LD see 5–20% higher Google-SERP-CTR.
13. **No alt-text on 30–50% of images = accessibility + SEO penalty.** The team uploads product images without alt-text, missing 30–50% of accessibility-and-SEO signal. **Fix:** by Phase 1 Day 4, alt-text is a required attribute per image in the canonical attribute-model. Per Akeneo 2024 + Salsify 2024 + Akeneo 2024 benchmarks, alt-text-on-every-image lifts SEO-traffic 5–15% + accessibility-compliance-pass-rate from 30–50% to 95–100%.
14. **No marketplace-suspension-monitor = 1–3 suspensions per year.** The brand has 1–3 Amazon / Walmart / TikTok Shop suspensions per year due to product-data-quality-issues (wrong-image / wrong-GTIN / wrong-price / wrong-category), each costing $5k–$50k in lost-revenue + reinstatement-fees. **Fix:** by Phase 3 Day 14, per-channel quality-gate + per-channel suspension-monitor + per-channel monthly-quality-report. Per ChannelEngine 2024 + Lengow 2024 + Feedonomics 2024 benchmarks, this reduces suspensions from 1–3 per year to 0.
15. **No cross-sell-graph = 0% PDP cross-sell-recommendation-quality.** The PDP shows generic "you might also like" recommendations that are not based on co-purchase-history or attribute-similarity. **Fix:** by Phase 1 Day 4, build cross-sell-graph in canonical attribute-model: related-product-1 / related-product-2 / cross-sell-1 / cross-sell-2 / up-sell-1 / bundle-component-1 / bundle-component-2. Per Nosto 2024 + Rebuy 2024 + Algolia 2024 benchmarks, attribute-graph-based-recommendation lifts CVR 5–15% vs generic-recommendation.
16. **No image-CDN budget guardrail = surprise $5k–$50k monthly bandwidth-bill.** The team enables Cloudinary / imgix / ImageKit without setting monthly-bandwidth-budget-guardrail, hitting $5k–$50k surprise-bills during BFCM. **Fix:** by Phase 2 Day 8, set monthly-bandwidth-budget-guardrail in image-CDN config + alert at 80% of budget + auto-throttle at 100% of budget. Per Cloudinary 2024 + imgix 2024 + ImageKit 2024 benchmarks, this prevents 100% of BFCM-bandwidth-bill-surprises.
17. **Per-channel mapping drift = 2–4 hours per channel per attribute-update.** The team updates a product attribute in Shopify and the change doesn't propagate to Amazon / Walmart / TikTok Shop / email-feed for 2–4 hours (or never). **Fix:** by Phase 3 Day 14, wire PIM → syndication → every channel with real-time-webhook + nightly-full-sync fallback. Per Salsify 2024 + ChannelEngine 2024 + Lengow 2024 + Feedonomics 2024 benchmarks, this reduces per-channel mapping-drift-time from 2–4 hours to <15 minutes.
18. **No brand-portal = 5+ hours/week of "where is the latest logo / brand-color / brand-font / brand-voice-guide" requests.** The brand-portal holds the canonical brand-assets (logo / color / font / voice-guide / approved-photography / approved-UGC); without it, the marketing / creative / wholesale / international / legal / finance teams each have their own copy in their own folder, drifting 5–30% per quarter. **Fix:** by Phase 2 Day 7, ship 5–25 brand-portals in DAM per brand-team (marketing / creative / e-commerce / wholesale / international / legal / finance). Per Bynder 2024 + Brandfolder 2024 + MediaValet 2024 benchmarks, this saves 5–25 hours/week of brand-asset-hunting time.
