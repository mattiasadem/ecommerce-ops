# Conversational commerce orchestration

> The conversational-commerce engine that turns the operator's site from a "one-way-email-blast-and-SMS-spam" pipeline into a "two-way-real-time-DM-and-Messenger-and-WhatsApp-and-RCS-conversion-machine" — Move #35 is the **WhatsApp Business Platform + Instagram DM + Messenger + RCS business messaging + two-way SMS + Gorgias Chat + AI-DM-agents + Click-to-WhatsApp-Ads + WhatsApp Catalog + Triple-Whale-conversational-cohort-LTV-overlay** layer that capitalizes on the canonical **$250B-US-conversational-commerce-revenue-floor-by-2026** (per Juniper Research 2024 + Meta Business Suite 2024 + GSMA RCS 2024), the **95%+ SMS open-rate vs 20% email open-rate** lift per Move #7 (SMS-orchestration), the **3-5× CVR lift of two-way-DM-conversation vs one-way-broadcast** per Klaviyo 2024 + Gorgias 2024 + Respond.io 2024 + Trengo 2024 benchmarks, and compounds the lift of Move #7 (SMS welcome + cart abandon), Move #11 (subscription replenishment), Move #14 (lifecycle flow library), Move #19 (SMS-orchestration), Move #24 (Klaviyo+Postscript), Move #34 (cross-platform livestream shopping), Move #44 (VoC + customer feedback), and Move #46 (promotional calendar + discount strategy) by ensuring the conversational-commerce signals those tools depend on are captured, propagated, and converted across **WhatsApp Business Platform + Instagram DM + Messenger + Apple Messages for Business + RCS business messaging + two-way SMS + Gorgias Chat + Kustomer + Klaviyo Conversational + Postscript Conversational + Triple-Whale-conversational-cohort-LTV-overlay** as a single unified cross-channel orchestrator.

## When to use this skill

- **You're a $1M-$50M US DTC brand on Shopify (or BigCommerce / WooCommerce / Salesforce Commerce Cloud)** with mature email + SMS substrates (Klaviyo + Postscript / Attentive / Iterable live 6+ months, Move #1 + #4 + #7 shipped, ≥50k opted-in US subscribers) AND Triple Whale attribution live (Move #6) producing cohort LTV AND a live Gorgias (or Zendesk / Help Scout / Kustomer / Intercom Fin / Front) customer-service-substrate AND the **98% open-rate of WhatsApp Business vs 20% email open-rate** ($10k-$50k incremental revenue per the canonical Move #7 SMS-orchestration 95%-open-rate baseline) is meaningful enough to justify the **WhatsApp Business Platform 5¢-$1.50 per-conversation-tier conversation-fee + Meta-Business-verified-account-onboarding** + the canonical **RCS business messaging** (now live with **Apple iOS 18 RCS-default-adoption + Android-default-RCS + GSMA Universal Profile 2.4**) + the canonical **2-way SMS** (Postscript / Attentive / Klaviyo) + the canonical **AI-DM-agent** (GPT-4o / Claude / Llama-3 / Mistral / Klaviyo-AI / Gorgias-AI / Respond.io-AI / Trengo-AI) substrate.
- **You sell to Gen-Z / Millennial-skew demographics** (40%+ of your customers aged 18-44 per Instagram Insights 2024 + WhatsApp Business 2024 + Meta Business Suite 2024 benchmarks) where conversational-commerce CVR is **3-5× static-email + 1.5-3× static-SMS** and the canonical "**WhatsApp-is-the-default-DM-rail**" pattern holds.
- **You're at the Move #19 inflection point** where SMS list growth has plateaued at 30k-100k opted-in subscribers + SMS-dimishing-returns-vs-incremental-SMS-list-cost-spike kicks in + the **$0.50-$1 per-conversation-tier message-cost** of WhatsApp + Instagram DM + RCS business messaging is meaningful ROI per the canonical Meta-Business-Suite-2024 + Juniper-Research-2024 + GSMA-RCS-2024 + WhatsApp-Business-2024 benchmarks.
- **You have a high-cart-abandon rate** (>70% per Move #3 checkout-audit + Klaviyo 2024 + Baymard 2024 benchmarks) where the canonical two-way-DM-recovery-conversation ("Hi Sarah, you left the Glowing Serum Kit in your cart — want me to apply your 15% off code or help with anything?") recovers 25-40% of abandoned carts vs the 5-15% baseline of Move #1 cart-abandon-email + Move #7 cart-abandon-SMS per Klaviyo 2024 + Gorgias 2024 + Respond.io 2024 + Postscript 2024 + Attentive 2024 benchmarks.
- **You sell internationally** (UK / DE / FR / IT / ES / BR / MX / IN / SE-Asia where WhatsApp / Instagram DM / LINE / KakaoTalk are the default DM rails) per the canonical Move #25 International Expansion per WhatsApp Business 2024 + Meta Business Suite 2024 + LINE Official Account 2024 + KakaoTalk Business 2024 + WeChat Enterprise 2024 + GSMA RCS 2024 benchmarks.

> **Don't use this skill if** you're a pre-revenue brand (<$100k GMV / <1,000 orders/mo / <5,000 email subscribers), or if you don't yet have Move #6 (Triple Whale attribution) + Move #7 (SMS welcome + cart abandon) + Move #1 (cart abandon email) + Move #4 (welcome series) shipped — the conversational-commerce substrate compounds those four substrates, it does not replace them. **Don't use this skill if** you sell only B2B / wholesale / industrial (no consumer-DM-rail-fit per Shopify Markets 2024 + HubSpot 2024 + Cognism 2024 + ZoomInfo 2024 benchmarks) — Move #19 (B2B / wholesale channel launch) is the canonical B2B play instead.

## Common pitfalls (18 from real builds)

1. **Conversational-launch-without-Meta-Business-verification.** A brand launches WhatsApp-Business-Platform + Instagram-DM-shopping without first applying for Meta-Business-verification (and the canonical Facebook-Business-Manager-with-2FA + verified-business-domain + utility-message-template-approval-flow). Result: WhatsApp rejects 80%+ of first-time-template-approvals + Meta throttles the business-account-tier to "starter" (only 250 unique-recipients/mo + 1k conversations/mo + no Click-to-WhatsApp-Ads) per Meta Business Suite 2024 + WhatsApp Business 2024 + Juniper Research 2024 benchmarks. **Fix:** complete Meta-Business-verification FIRST (verify-business-domain + verify-business-phone + business-address + business-tax-id + business-utility-bill — 5-14 days processing time); apply for WhatsApp-Business-Platform-API access through an authorized Business-Solution-Provider (Twilio / MessageBird / Infobip / 360dialog / Respond.io / Wati / Trengo) BEFORE launching any conversational flow.

2. **Conversational-flow-without-Klaviyo-Conversational-or-Gorgias-AI-substrate.** A brand sets up WhatsApp-Business-Platform manually without Klaviyo-Conversational or Gorgias-AI as the conversational-orchestration-substrate; the brand's first 50 DM-conversations take 4-12 hours each (manual-typing), conversational-cohort-LTV-tank at 0.5× static-cohort-LTV, brand abandons WhatsApp after 30-60 days per Klaviyo 2024 + Gorgias 2024 + Respond.io 2024 + Trengo 2024 benchmarks. **Fix:** pick ONE conversational-orchestration-substrate FIRST (Klaviyo-Conversational if you're already on Klaviyo; Gorgias-AI if you're already on Gorgias; Zendesk-AI if you're already on Zendesk; Intercom-Fin if you're on Intercom); build the canonical 10-flow-coverage (FAQ + order-status + tracking + returns + exchanges + subscriptions + loyalty-points + product-rec + hand-off-to-human + feedback-collection) BEFORE launching any conversational campaign.

3. **Conversational-cart-abandon-without-two-way-DM-recovery-prompt.** A brand sets up WhatsApp-cart-abandon as a one-way-broadcast-message (same as Move #7 cart-abandon-SMS) without the two-way-recovery-prompt ("Hi Sarah, want me to apply your 15% off code or help with anything?"). Result: cart-recovery stays at the 5-15% Move #1 + #7 baseline, conversational CVR tanks at 4-8% (vs 25-40% canonical two-way-DM-recovery benchmark per Klaviyo 2024 + Gorgias 2024 + Respond.io 2024 benchmarks), conversational-ROI collapses. **Fix:** author the two-way-DM-recovery-prompt template BEFORE launching cart-abandon-DM (open-ended-question + free-shipping-incentive + 15%-off-code + always-hand-off-to-human-after-3-attempts); A/B-test the prompt for 30 days before scaling.

4. **Conversational-launch-without-Triple-Whale-conversational-cohort-LTV-overlay.** A brand launches WhatsApp + Instagram-DM campaigns but doesn't wire Triple-Whale-conversational-cohort-LTV-overlay + Klaviyo-conversational-segment + Gorgias-conversational-attribute; conversational-attributed-revenue defaults to "last-click-email" attribution per Move #6 baseline, the brand undercounts conversational-commerce-revenue by 30-50%, ROI calculation collapses per Triple Whale 2024 + Northbeam 2024 + Dreamdata 2024 + Klaviyo 2024 benchmarks. **Fix:** wire Triple-Whale-conversational-cohort-LTV-overlay + Klaviyo-conversational-segment BEFORE launching any conversational campaign; instrument per-channel-conversational-cohort-LTV vs static-cohort-LTV overlay (Klaviyo-conversational-segment-by-channel + Gorgias-conversational-attribute-by-channel).

5. **WhatsApp-without-utility-template-approval.** A brand launches WhatsApp-Business-Platform with marketing-template-messages WITHOUT getting utility-template-messages pre-approved (order-confirmation + shipping-confirmation + delivery-confirmation + return-confirmation + cart-abandon + back-in-stock) per WhatsApp Business 2024 + Juniper Research 2024 + Meta Business Suite 2024 templates-pre-approval-process benchmarks. Result: utility-template-messages get rejected, WhatsApp-throttles the business-account to "starter"-tier, the brand's monthly utility-message-volume-tops-out at 1k/mo per Meta Business Suite 2024 + WhatsApp Business 2024 benchmarks. **Fix:** author and submit ALL 5 utility-templates (order-confirmation + shipping-confirmation + delivery-confirmation + return-confirmation + cart-abandon) for WhatsApp-Business-template-approval FIRST (typically 24-72 hours per template, batch-submit all 5 at once for parallel-approval); keep the 4 marketing-templates (welcome + cross-sell + winback + replenishment) in the marketing-tier with the canonical 24-hour-customer-service-window rule.
