- **<$3M GMV OR <$20k/mo marketing-budget OR <1 retailer-relationship-already-active.** Self-serve retail-media-platforms require minimum-budget-thresholds ($500-$5,000/mo per network) AND minimum-team-capacity (0.25-0.5 FTE) to operate. - **Wholesale-channel-or-DTC-only brands without retailer-relationship.** Retail-media platforms require the brand to be an authorized-1P or 3P-seller OR a vendor-onboarded-via-Partner-Network at the retailer. Brands without this CPG-grade-substrate cannot operate 1P-or-DSV-or-hardlines-onboarding.
- **B2B-only / wholesale-only brands.** B2B/wholesale channels (Move #14.5) use Faire + Tundra + Ankorstore + Handshake + RSP/KeHE/UNFI — not retail-media-platforms. If the brand has zero DTC AND zero marketplace-presence, defer Move #47.
- **Retailer-PII-restricted verticals (alcohol, firearm, tobacco, cannabis, Rx-pharma, politically-sensitive-CPG).** Most retail-media-platforms (Walmart, Target, Kroger, Instacart, Amazon) restrict alcohol/firearm/tobacco/cannabis/Rx-pharma to opt-out-only AND ban the brand from sponsored-product-listings on those SKUs. Confirm advertiser-eligibility per retailer per SKU-vertical before committing spend.
- **Cross-border-PII-restricted verticals (EU-GDPR + UK-DPA + CA-PIPEDA).** Walmart Canada + Walmart-Mexico + ASDA-UK + Ahold-Delhaize EU operate under cross-border-PII-restrictions; off-platform-extension across borders requires explicit GDPR-compliant-data-clean-room (Snowflake-Datacleaner-AWS-Clean-Rooms-Hair-Room-Double-Cloud-Room) and is out-of-scope for Path A.
- **Pure-content-publishers (news/editorial/music/UGC-video).** Retail-media is fundamentally for physical-product-CPG-or-DTC-CPG-or-hardlines; pure-content-publishers should use Yelp-ads + Spotify-ads + TikTok-ads + Meta-Audience-Network instead.
